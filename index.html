Welcome

I'm a PhD candidate (exp. grad. '19) of the Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Department at the University of Minnesota, under the supervision of Prof. Georgios B. Giannakis.

My research interests span the areas of Machine Learning, Statistical Signal Processing, and Optimization. Specifically, I am interested in design of robust and efficient algorithmic frameworks for machine learning tasks. I am currently focused on robust learning for deep neural networks, and particularly on uncertainty estimation in adversarial settings for reliable utilization of DNNs.

Please see Research and my Google Scholar for a complete list of publications.
